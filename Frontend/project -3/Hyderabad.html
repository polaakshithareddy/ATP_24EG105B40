Hyderabd

Hyderabad is a major metropolitan area in Telangana, India, officially spanning across four districts: Hyderabad, Medchal-Malkajgiri, Ranga Reddy, and Sangareddy. Key areas and divisions include the core Hyderabad city, the twin city of Secunderabad, and the IT hub of Cyberabad, along with numerous prominent residential, commercial, and historical neighbourhoods.
